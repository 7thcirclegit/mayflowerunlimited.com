Postcards


Mayflower Unlimited produces postcards designed to aid you in realizing your intrinsic right to liberty and how it applies to your life. Our aim is to inform, entertain, and inspire.

Andrew Von Sonn created and illustrated a collection of 52 postcards you can see with the links below. After his (unexpected) passing in October 2015, all of Mayflower Unlimited's inventory was carefully placed in storage with plans to make it available again the future.

Please contact us to be informed when Andrew Von Sonn's postcards are made available again.

Each postcard measures 4.25" x 5.50". (Note: Some images are also available on our Bad Taste T-Shirts.)
